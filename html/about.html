Our story

Crafted by Win blossomed into existence as a vibrant start-up enterprise, its petals unfurling on the entrepreneurial landscape with a debut on February 2024. Founded by the visionary Winnie P. Garlan, fueled by passion and creativity, Crafted by Win embarked on its journey to redefine the artistry of handcrafted satin ribbon flowers.

CBW embarked on its journey to redefine the artistry of handcrafted satin ribbon flowers. With a mission to spread love and joy through meticulously crafted floral arrangements, the fledgling business set its sights on becoming a beacon of elegance and thoughtfulness in the Philippines.

Supported by co-founder Rey-an G. Garlan and a dedicated team of artisans, Crafted by Win quickly gained traction, captivating hearts with its exquisite creations.

From its humble beginnings, Crafted by Win embraced innovation, blending traditional craftsmanship with contemporary flair to cultivate a signature style that captivated customers across Metro Manila.

As the petals of success unfurled, Crafted by Win continued to bloom, weaving its way into the tapestry of special moments and celebrations, one satin ribbon flower at a time.
